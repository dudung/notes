+ Rearrange of (I5) will produce
$$\tag{I6}
\begin{array}{rcl}
2a(x - x_0) & = & (v_0 + v)(v - v_0) \\\\[0.5em]
(v + v_0)(v - v_0) & = & 2a(x - x_0) \\\\[0.5em]
v^2 - v_0^2 & = & 2a(x - x_0) \\\\[0.5em]
v^2 & = & v_0^2 + 2a(x - x_0),
\end{array}
$$
which is the third kinematics equation.
