$$\tag{1}
\Delta x = \tfrac12 (v_1 + v_2) \Delta t
$$

$$\tag{2}
v_2 = v_1 + a \Delta t
$$

$$\tag{3}
\Delta x = v_1 \Delta t + \tfrac12 a t^2
$$

$$\tag{4}
v_2^2 = v_1^2 + 2 a \Delta x
$$

$$\tag{5}
\Delta x = v_2 \Delta t - \tfrac12 a t^2
$$
