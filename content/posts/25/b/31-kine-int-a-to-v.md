+ Integrate acceleration $a$ to obtain velocity $v$
$$\tag{E1}
v(t) - v(t_0) = \int_{t_0}^t a(\tau) d\tau.
$$
+ Initial condition $v(t_0) = v_0$, will give
$$\tag{E2}
v(t) = v_0 + \int_{t_0}^t a(\tau) d\tau.
$$
