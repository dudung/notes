+ Only three equations, which are
$$\tag{A1}
v_t = v_0 + a t,
$$
$$\tag{A2}
x_t = x_0 + v_0 t + \tfrac12 a t^2,
$$
$$\tag{A3}
v_t^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x_t - x_0).
$$
+ There are other two equations, that can be obtained by substitution of (1) to (2) in order to eliminate $a$ or $v_0$.
