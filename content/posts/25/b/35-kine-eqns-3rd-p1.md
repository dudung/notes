+ First two equations, with $v = v(t)$ and $x = x(t)$, are
$$\tag{I1}
v = v_0 + at,
$$
$$\tag{I2}
x = x_0 + v_0 t +  \tfrac12 a t^2.
$$
+ Turn (I1) into following 
$$\tag{I3}
t = \frac{v - v_0}{a}.
$$
and then substitute to (I2)
