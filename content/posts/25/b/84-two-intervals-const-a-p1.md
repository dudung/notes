+ Acceleration
$$\tag{D1}
a(t) = \left\\{
\begin{array}{cc}
a_0, & t_0 \le t < t_1, \\\\[0.5em]
a_1, & t_1 \le t < t_2,
\end{array}
\right.
$$
with $t_{n+1} = t_n + \tau_n$, where $\tau_n$ is $n$-th time interval.
